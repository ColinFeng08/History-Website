Location

Located off the coast of Spain and France, Petria has been a strong dictatorship since the end of World War II. Petria is scattered with mountains, and lying between a valley is its capital - Kamru.


Population

The current population of Petria is 40 million people, Petria is one of the most diverse countries in Europe, due to the immigration during World War Two. The population is quite diverse, with 90% of the older generation being not native to the island.


Language

The locals of Petria of are very flexable with most people being able to speak three languages fluently - English, Spainish and French. This is the result of the mass imigration during WW2 which made the country very diverse.
